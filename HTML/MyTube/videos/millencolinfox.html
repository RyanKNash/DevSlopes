Millencolin delivering the light-hearted and fun side of punk.

Millencolin is a Swedish punk rock band that was formed on 12 October 1992 by Nikola Šarčević, Mathias Färm, and Erik Ohlsson in Örebro, Sweden.
In early 1993, drummer Fredrik Larzon joined the band.
The name Millencolin is derived from the skateboard trick "melancholy".
